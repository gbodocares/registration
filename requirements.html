Photography is the art and science of capturing images using light. It involves recording scenes or subjects by means of a camera, which uses light to form an image on a medium such as film or a digital sensor. The word "photography" comes from the Greek words phos (meaning "light") and graphé (meaning "drawing" or "writing"), so it essentially means "drawing with light."
